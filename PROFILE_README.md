### My Approach

Most companies don't need another platform. They need the platform they already have to do more.

I specialize in designing and building **backend AI services** that plug into existing systems — no migrations, no vendor lock-in, no disruption. Think of it as adding an intelligence layer to infrastructure that already works.

```
  Your System  ──▶  AI Backend I Build  ──▶  Your System, Now Smarter
  (EHR, CRM,        (processes, classifies,    (structured data, automation,
   LIMS, etc.)       validates, extracts)        better decisions)
```

I bring a dual perspective to every project: the **business analyst** who maps stakeholder needs, compliance requirements, and ROI — and the **engineer** who builds the API, writes the tests, and ships the container.

**[DocIQ](https://github.com/pierremontanov/dociq)** — A backend AI engine that converts unstructured medical PDFs (scanned or digital, English or Spanish) into clean, validated, FHIR-compliant JSON. Built to plug into existing healthcare infrastructure without replacing anything.

Prescriptions, lab results, clinical histories — in as a PDF, out as structured data your EHR, LIMS, or patient portal already understands.

**Stack:** Python, FastAPI, Tesseract OCR, OpenCV, Pydantic v2, FHIR R4, Docker &nbsp;|&nbsp; **1,100+ tests** &nbsp;|&nbsp; Production-ready with Nginx, rate limiting, and health probes.
